Nordic Legends

Ragnar Lothbrok

Ragnar Lothbrok a norse legened responsible for reaking havoc on the

saxon english settlers of 8th century europe. He was eventually captured

by king Aella during one of his raids and was fed to a pit of snakes.

Later all of his sons would come with a great viking army to avenge

their father and where successful in doing so.

They then performed a blood eagle on king Aella one of the most gruesome

of nordic rituals.
